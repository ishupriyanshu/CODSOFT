Welcome to Hogwarts

Welcome to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, the finest magical institution in the wizarding world. Founded over a thousand years ago by the four greatest witches and wizards of the age, Hogwarts has been a beacon of magical education ever since.

At Hogwarts, students are sorted into one of four houses: Gryffindor, Slytherin, Ravenclaw, or Hufflepuff. Each house has its own unique qualities and values, fostering a sense of belonging and camaraderie among its members.

Explore the tabs below to learn more about each house and discover the magic that awaits!

Gryffindor

Gryffindor is one of the four Houses of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Its emblematic animal is the lion and its colors are scarlet and gold. The head of this house is the Transfiguration Professor and Deputy Headmistress, Minerva McGonagall, and the house ghost is Sir Nicholas de Mimsy-Porpington, more commonly known as Nearly Headless Nick.

Slytherin

Slytherin is one of the four Houses of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Its emblematic animal is the serpent and its colors are green and silver. The head of this house is the Potions Master and Head of Slytherin House, Severus Snape, and the house ghost is the Bloody Baron.

Ravenclaw

Ravenclaw is one of the four Houses of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Its emblematic animal is the eagle and its colors are blue and bronze. The head of this house is the Charms professor, Filius Flitwick, and the house ghost is the Grey Lady, otherwise known as Helena Ravenclaw.

Hufflepuff

Hufflepuff is one of the four Houses of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Its emblematic animal is the badger, and Yellow and Black are its colors. The head of this house is the Herbology Professor, Pomona Sprout, and the house ghost is The Fat Friar.
